About Me

I'm Nzioki Dennis, a freelance developer with a passion for software development and aviation. My journey in the world of technology and flight has been an exciting one.

As a software developer, I enjoy creating innovative solutions and exploring the ever-evolving landscape of programming languages and technologies.

Simultaneously, my fascination with aviation fuels my desire to learn about aerodynamics, aircraft design, and the principles that allow us to defy gravity.

Through my writings, I aim to share insights, knowledge, and the joy I find in these two distinct yet captivating worlds of software development and aviation.
